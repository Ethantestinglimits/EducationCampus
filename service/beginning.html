BEGINNING

Everything started when we passed our formation. We were really happy to finally be able to work with our diploma. We get out for a drink, and we end up talking about the country where people couldn’t access education and diplomas. The idea appeared here : what if we create an association to help these countries ? This happened several years ago and the association has been created and started growing. The project is now a solid foundation, but we lack resources and contacts, and you can help us about it.

Basically, we had the idea to allow defavorised children access education by creating an association, and we now need your contribution.

Education is the key to unlock our free will and mind Let’s free them !
