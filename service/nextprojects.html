OUR NEXT PROJECTS

Our first campus is based in Togo, indeed it's a backward country with a low education rate. Moreover, it’s a country that is easy to access and currently has no political problems. This is why it’s the lucky one for our first intervention.

But our ambitions don’t stop there, in fact, in the future we wish to operate in other African countries such as Somalia, which suffers extremely at the educational level, but also in the whole world by acting in Asia, or even in Latin America.

You should know that the more support we receive, the more we, and thousands of children, can dream bigger.
